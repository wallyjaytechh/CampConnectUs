About CampConnectUs

Our Mission

CampConnectUs is revolutionizing campus communication by bridging the gap between students and staff. We combine the instant messaging convenience of WhatsApp with the social networking power of Facebook, creating a unified platform designed specifically for educational institutions.

Our Vision

To become the leading campus communication platform in Africa, connecting millions of students and educators in a secure, engaging, and productive digital environment that enhances learning, collaboration, and campus life.

Why We Started

Founded in 2024 by Wally Jay, CampConnectUs was born from a simple observation: students and staff needed a better way to communicate. Existing social platforms were either too informal or not designed for educational needs. We created a solution that understands the unique dynamics of campus life.

Meet Our Founder

Wally Jay - A visionary entrepreneur passionate about education technology. With years of experience in both education and software development, Wally identified the communication gaps in educational institutions and dedicated himself to building a solution that truly serves students and staff alike.
